Our Services

Clients always look for the right service with quality and value for money and this has been our leading edge over these years of existence into engineering services.

We leverage our skills to support our customers in the following areas:

Executed over 20,000 MT of Structural Works. Structural Steel & Pipeline fabrication and erection are our core business services. Our fabrication team is fully equipped and highly experienced to deliver the service as per the contractual requirements. We work closely with our customers and take the minutest detail to ensure that we outline the job site controls for structural steel fabrication and erection to meet the project requirements.

Executed over 10,000 MT of Technological Structures Jobs. We take the specifications in conjunction to the scope of the works and perform the requirement of all operations necessary for fabrication and erection of technological structures. Our technical experts design the engineering practice which are in line with the required safety, material choice, constructability, operability and its maintainability in accordance to the approvals of our engineers.

Executed over 10,000 MT of Equipment Installation Works. Our engineers do the detailing of the applicable installation and erection of the mechanical equipments. We carry out execution of Millwright fitters activities and have a well defined process for fitter and riggers process for supporting all types of mechanical equipment erections. The commissioning of major pieces of equipments and its installation is carried out as per the suppliers or the contractors detailed procedure. We create flexible solutions for our clients so that we are able to cater to projects no matter how complex or challenging it is.

Executed over 5,000 MT of Dismantling, Re-built, Shutdown & Maintenance Works. We are well outfitted to address any plant shutdown activities, structural modification works and plant maintenance projects as we follow a coordinated work relationship with our people and all other associated staffs like millwright fitters, pipefitters, welders, engineers and technicians for high quality deliverables and performance optimization. We allocate proper time and resources for any plant shutdown and maintenance projects so that the end product from the commissioning of rebuilt systems or the delivered project results in fruitful outcome for maintaining the health of the system for years to come.

Executed over 1000 MT of PEBs, Painting of over 20,000 MT of Structures, Sheeting over 1,50,000 Square Meters for Structural Buildings. We also provide services like Pre-Engineered Buildings designing, construction and commissioning, Painting with shot/sand/grit blasting for any structural & pipeline projects and Sheeting Projects by ensuring safe working environment and adherence to relevant regulations.
